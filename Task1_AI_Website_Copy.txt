Task 1 – AI Website Copy Generator
Prompt Used:
Act as a professional website copywriter. Create website content for a local coffee shop named Brew Haven. Include homepage introduction, about section, services offered, and a call to action. The tone should be friendly and modern, targeting students and young professionals.
AI Generated Output:
Homepage Introduction:
Welcome to Brew Haven Coffee Shop, a cozy and comfortable place where customers can enjoy freshly brewed premium coffee in a relaxing environment. It is the perfect spot for students and professionals to refresh, work, and socialize.
About Us:
Brew Haven focuses on delivering high-quality coffee made from carefully selected beans. We aim to create a warm and inviting atmosphere where customers can unwind and enjoy great taste along with excellent customer service.
Services Offered:
• Variety of hot and cold coffee beverages
• Fresh snacks and bakery items
• Free Wi-Fi and comfortable seating
Call To Action:
Visit Brew Haven today and experience the perfect blend of taste, comfort, and productivity.
Prompt Objective:
The objective of this prompt is to generate structured and engaging website content for a local business using clear instructions and defined content sections.
Learning Outcome:
Through this task, I understood how effective prompt design helps artificial intelligence tools generate relevant, organized, and user-friendly business content for practical applications.
