Task 3 – AI SEO Blog Content Generator
Prompt Used:
Act as an SEO content writer. Write a simple and informative blog article on the topic “Benefits of Drinking Coffee for Productivity”. Include a clear title, introduction, key benefits, and a short conclusion. The tone should be informative and student-friendly.
AI Generated Output:
Title: Benefits of Drinking Coffee for Productivity
Introduction:
Coffee is one of the most popular beverages consumed worldwide. Many students and professionals prefer coffee during study or work sessions because it helps them stay active and focused.
Key Benefits:
• Improves concentration and mental alertness
• Boosts energy levels during long study hours
• Helps reduce tiredness and improves mood
• Supports better work performance when consumed in moderation
Conclusion:
Drinking coffee in moderate amounts can support productivity and focus, especially for students and working individuals who need sustained energy throughout the day.
